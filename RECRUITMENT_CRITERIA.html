RECRUITMENT CRITERIA

We select candidates based on skills, experience, and integrity to ensure
they meet global employment standards.

Manpower
Requirements

Please send the required number of workers for each job category or position, along with the salary offered.

Job Descriptions

Clearly define the qualifications and experience required for each job category.

Work Location Details

Provide the job location, site/project area, and nature of the job in detail so candidates can be accurately briefed prior to recruitment.

Air Tickets

Will the company provide air tickets for joining employment? Are return tickets available for annual leave?

Accommodation

Describe the accommodation facilities offered. If accommodation is not provided, is there any allowance?

Food Facilities

Are meals provided? If not, is there a meal allowance? Alternatively, are self-cooking facilities available?

Medical Facilities

Does the company provide free medical facilities (e.g., a medical card)? If not, is there a medical allowance?

Working Hours

Working hours/day, working days/week, off days/month, shift duty requirement, and inclusion of lunch & prayer breaks.

Resignation Conditions

If an employee resigns or is terminated, what are the terms for repatriation, final account settlement, and other related procedures?
