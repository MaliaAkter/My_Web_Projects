About MP Travels LTD.

MP Travels Ltd., a proud sister concern of Samaira Group, is one of Bangladesh’s most reputable and government-licensed overseas employment agencies (RL-1128), approved by the Ministry of Labor and Employment. Based in the heart of Dhaka, we are led by industry experts with over 15 years of experience and supported by a skilled, well-trained team operating from a modern, fully equipped office.
We specialize in providing professionally skilled, semi-skilled, and unskilled Bangladeshi workers to the Middle East (KSA, UAE, Qatar, Kuwait, Oman, Bahrain), Asian countries (Malaysia, Singapore, Cambodia, Taiwan, Vietnam), and recently Japan. Our recruitment covers diverse sectors including engineering, security, hospitality, manufacturing, administration, oil & gas, and more.
Guided by smart work, honesty, and efficiency, our mission is to connect the right talent with the right opportunities—on time and with excellence. We are committed to building long-term partnerships based on trust and mutual success.

MISSION

To connect Bangladeshi talent with global opportunities by delivering ethical recruitment services. We match candidates with employers through structured processes, skill enhancement, and commitment to honesty and professionalism.

VISION

To be the trusted and preferred overseas employment agency in Bangladesh, recognized globally for excellence in manpower recruitment, client satisfaction, and reducing unemployment through sustainable international job placements.
