RECRUITMENT PROCESS

A streamlined selection procedure ensuring the right talent is matched with the right opportunity.

MP Travels Ltd. Upon receiving the authorized Demand Letter from the respective company, MP Travels Limited submits the documents to the Bureau of Manpower Employment and Training (BMET) for pre-labor approval.

Once pre-approved by BMET, the Demand Letter is advertised in national newspapers. MP Travels Limited also uses digital platforms and SMS to reach candidates.

We review our up-to-date database to identify potential candidates based on their qualifications, skills, and experience according to employer requirements.

Candidates are informed about the job details and initial screening process through direct communication.

Shortlisted candidates are sent for a full medical examination at government-authorized medical centers.

We compile and send all required documents for visa processing—such as passport copies and medical reports—to the employer.

Once candidates receive the job offer, we conduct an orientation session to help create awareness and build a strong relationship.

All necessary documents are submitted to BMET for final labor approval and immigration clearance.

All departments at MP Travels Limited are fully computerized to ensure fast and reliable service for flight bookings and travel coordination.

MP Travels Ltd. is a sister concern of Samaira Group and one of the most reputable overseas employment agencies in Bangladesh.
